About Brittany Morgante

Decoding Dogs Training: Unleashing the Power of Communication

My Story

I started Decoding Dogs Training because I wanted to create the training program that I wish I could access when I was first struggling with my dog back in 2012.

Like many dog owners, I found myself overwhelmed and unsure of how to handle the challenges that came with my dog, Myra. I needed step-by-step guidance, clear answers, and someone who understood what I was going through. That experience shaped everything about how I approach dog training today.

In 2017, I began offering private training lessons to friends and family, and quickly realized that many dog owners were facing the same struggles I had experienced. Since then, I've dedicated myself to helping owners build better relationships with their dogs through effective communication and balanced training methods.

My Training Philosophy

Balanced Training Approach

I believe in using what works for each individual dog. This means incorporating a variety of humane, effective training tools and methods—including relationship building, play, motivation, and e-collar training—to help your dog succeed.

Communication is Key

Training is all about teaching you and your dog how to communicate clearly with each other. When you both understand what's expected, living together becomes easier and more enjoyable.

Customized for Your Dog

Every dog has a unique personality, learning style, and set of challenges. I create personalized training plans that fit your dog's needs and your specific goals—not a one-size-fits-all approach.

No Judgment, Just Support

I've been where you are. I understand the frustration, the confusion, and the feeling that you're failing your dog. You're not. My job is to support you, answer your questions, and help you succeed—without any judgment.

What Makes Me Different

Real-World Experience

I've been training dogs professionally for several years, but more importantly, I've lived the challenges that come with difficult dog behaviors. My approach comes from both professional expertise and personal understanding.

Ongoing Education

Dog training is constantly evolving, and I'm committed to staying current. I regularly attend continuing education opportunities to bring you the most effective, up-to-date training methods.

Focus on Relationship Building

Training isn't just about obedience—it's about building a strong, trusting relationship between you and your dog. When that foundation is in place, everything else falls into place more naturally.

Practical, Realistic Training

I don't believe in making promises I can't keep. I'll be honest with you about what's achievable, how long it will take, and what you need to do to see results. Success requires your commitment and daily practice.

My Core Values

Empathy

I understand the emotional side of dog ownership—the stress, the love, and the desire to do right by your dog.

Effectiveness

I use methods that work and deliver real, lasting results for you and your dog.

Education

I believe in empowering owners with knowledge so you understand not just what to do, but why.

Honesty

I'll always give you straightforward, judgment-free guidance and realistic expectations.

Beyond Training

When I'm not working with clients, you can find me spending time with my own dogs and continuing to learn and grow as a trainer. I'm passionate about helping dogs and their owners live happier, more harmonious lives together.
