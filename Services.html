Training Assignments 

Success in private lessons starts with you! Just like personal fitness training, dog training requires consistency and commitment. Attending a session once a week isnt enoughreal progress comes from daily practice and reinforcement at home!
 

* Your trainer will leave you with a training plan between each lesson

* It is important to practice training regularly with your dog during your program.

What You Can Expect From Training

Living with your dog shouldn't feel like a burden. Our approach is focused on making sure that everyone involved in training feels heard and listened to. Your trainer will always answer any questions you have in detail and without judgment.

 

You will feel more in-control of your dog's behaviors, and have a way to communicate with your dog that simply makes sense.

What Can We Help With?

Foundational Obedience

Sit, down, off, come, drop it, leave it

Relationship Building

Build a strong bond with your dog by learning how to set and maintain boundaries, communicate, and play

Impulse Control 

Learn how to teach your dog an on and off switch to help regulate their hyper activity and settle in the house

Play +

Play is a critical element to building a bond and relationship with your dog. Your trainer will help to find what motivates your dog to use in training. Each dog has different preferences, but categories of play incorporate food, toys, and personal play

Off Leash 

Learn the effective and humane way to use an ecollar to unlock a world of freedom for your dog. The ecollar is a highly effective communication tool when used knowledgeably 

Body Language

Your trainer will educate you on everything your dog is saying through their body language. Facial expressions, posture, tail position/wag, and movements - can all indicate the emotions your dog is feeling
