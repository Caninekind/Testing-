Frequently Asked Questions

What happens after I submit this form?

We'll review your submission and contact you within 24-48 hours to schedule your FREE 15-minute phone consultation. During this call, we'll discuss your dog's needs and determine if private training is the right fit.

Do I need to purchase a training package right away?

No! You'll start with a first lesson ($135 for 1.5 hours) before committing to a package. This allows us to assess your dog and create a customized plan together.

Where do training sessions take place?

All training sessions are conducted in your home. This allows us to work with your dog in their natural environment and address real-world challenges.

What if I'm not sure if training will work for my dog?

That's completely normal! The free phone consultation and first lesson are designed to help you feel confident about moving forward. We'll be honest with you about what's realistic and achievable.
